      My expertise

      I am a Junior Web Developer and Designer with a background in graphic design and customer service, combining creative expertise with strong problem-solving and teamwork skills to deliver visually engaging and functional websites and web apps.

      Originally from France, I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design from Université Laval in Canada, where I developed skills in graphic design, web design, and UI/UX design. After working for two years as a graphic designer in an agency and as a freelancer, I moved to Germany and gained valuable experience in customer service. Among these roles, I worked for Facebook's advertising services, building expertise in e-commerce and online marketing.

      Recently, I completed an intensive 6-month Full-Stack Web Development program at CareerFoundry, where I honed my technical skills to complement my design background. This unique combination enables me to create websites and applications that are not only functional but also aesthetically compelling and user-focused.
